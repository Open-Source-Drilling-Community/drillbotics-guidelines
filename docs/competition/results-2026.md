# 2026 Drillbotics competition results

!!! info "Official announcement"
    The canonical public announcement is published on Drillbotics.com: [Drillbotics 2026 Competition Results](https://drillbotics.com/drillbotics-2026-competition-results/).

The 2026 Drillbotics competition cycle is complete. Congratulations to all participating teams for their work across the physical and virtual modes.

## Results

| Mode | Recognition | Team | Country/region |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mode Physical (P) | 1st place | Instituto Tecnológico de Buenos Aires (ITBA) | Argentina |
| Mode Physical (P) | Joint 2nd place | Clausthal University of Technology (TU Clausthal) | Germany |
| Mode Physical (P) | Joint 2nd place | Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) | Norway |
| Mode Virtual (V) | 1st place | Palawan State University / Sunway University collaboration | Philippines/Malaysia |
| Mode Virtual (V) | Runner-up | UMaT Well Control | Ghana |

## Conference paper invitation

ITBA has been invited to prepare the 2026 Drillbotics conference paper and present the winning work at the SPE Drilling Conference, March 9-11, 2027, in Stavanger, Norway.

## Looking ahead

The 2027 Drillbotics rules will be published later in 2026, with notable changes expected. Teams interested in the next cycle should watch the official Drillbotics site and this guidelines site for updates.
