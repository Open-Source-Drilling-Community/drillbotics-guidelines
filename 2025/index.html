    New Team Checklist¶

    Use this quick checklist to set up your team. See the linked pages for details.

    • Confirm eligibility, advisors, and team composition — Overview
    • Choose your track — Group A or Group B
    • Register and note all deadlines — Timeline
    • Set up your repo and communication channels — Contributor Guide
    • Review deliverables and scoring — Rules & Scoring
    • Prepare your environment
      Group A: OpenLab + D‑WIS; Group B: rig, sensors, and safety procedures — Safety
    • Submit Phase I materials; schedule Phase II planning — Deliverables pages
